1. Roll two six-sided dice.
2. On first roll
    a. If you get a 7 or 11 you win!
    b. If you get a 2, 3, or 12 you lose!
    c. Any other number you don't win or lose. The die roll becomes your 'point.'
3. Keep rolling the dice again until:
    a. You roll the point again and win!
    b. or you roll a 7 and lose.
Good luck!
